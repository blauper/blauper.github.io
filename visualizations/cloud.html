Cloudiness

There appear to be a large number of cloudless cities near the equator. There also appear to be more cities located in the northern hemisphere that display cloudy conditions, which makes sense given January is a cooler month in the north. However, there are also several cloudless cities in the north, and plenty of cloudy ones in the south.
